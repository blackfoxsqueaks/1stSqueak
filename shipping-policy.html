Shipping Policy

Last Updated: 11/12/2025

This policy details how we deliver physical products, such as Signals QR Stickers, to our users across Egypt.

Drop currently ships to all major governorates in the Arab Republic of Egypt. Shipping availability and fees are calculated at checkout based on your specific location.

Orders are processed within 24 hours of confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.

• Cairo, Giza, & New Cairo: 2 - 3 business days.
• Alexandria & Nile Delta: 3 - 4 business days.
• Upper Egypt & Remote Areas: 5 - 7 business days.

Shipping fees are non-refundable once an order has been dispatched. If a package is returned to us due to an incorrect address provided by the user, a re-shipping fee may apply.

Once your order is handed over to our delivery partner, you will receive a tracking number via the Drop App or SMS. You can use this number to track your order.
