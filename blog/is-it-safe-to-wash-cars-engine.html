Should I Clean My Car’s Engine?
The engine of your car needs to be cleaned for a number of reasons. It gives an automobile the appearance of being well-kept and cared for, which might increase the resale value. In addition, some experts claim that an engine can run cooler if it is clean and free of grease, oil, or other contaminates. In other words, it may contribute to the reduction of overheating.

Today, we’ll investigate if cleaning an engine actually has any mechanical advantages.
Many people are curious as to whether cleaning an engine has any technical advantages. The quick response to this question is no, not really. The main benefit of cleaning an engine is preventing the buildup of dirt, debris, and sludge. The engine is subsequently kept cooler as a result, although an overheated engine will not be cooled down by this. You must examine the cooling system for it. So a cleaner engine will not have any mechanical improvements but it can save you from expensive and time-consuming repairs.
The short is answer is Yes, and No!
Then, How often should the engine of a car be cleaned?
There is no set time limit for cleaning a car’s engine. Here, the location of your car’s daily parking spot and the local climate are very important. For example, residents of dusty places might need to clean their engines more frequently than residents of cleaner areas.

Simply put, as Cairo residents where there is a lot of dirt and debris on the roadways you should strive to clean your car’s engine roughly every three months. While residents of other cities such as Alexandria or Portsaid, where the climate is less dry, and more humid. They should clean their car’s engine at least twice a year.
What is the best way to clean the engine?
A car’s engine has lots of different materials, and parts, thus requires the most exquisite yet careful care. Using water to clean the engine is a sure way to damage electrical components, and dry out gaskets. Some workshops may use petroleum derivates such as thinner to drop composited oils off your car’s engine, but this drys out the gaskets even more, and makes your engine more prone to collect dirt in the future.

The best way to clean an engine is to use steam along with a degreasing chemical. As steam when used at the proper temperature and angle, steam is effective at removing dirt that has become firmly embedded between tiny crevices. As opposed to high-pressure water sprayers and petroleum-derivates , it does not harm delicate engine components like electrical wiring and sensors, making it safer as well. In addition, steam cleaning is more cost-effective because it uses a small fraction of the water than a typical wash would.
