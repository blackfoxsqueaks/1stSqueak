Refund Policy

Last Updated: 11/12/2025

This policy outlines the terms for refunds and cancellations across our On-Demand services, Subscriptions, and Workshop bookings.

For single-service WashDrop bookings:
• Free Cancellation: You may cancel your booking at any time before the service provider arrives at your vehicle location.
• Non-Refundable: Once the service has been completed and confirmed, payments are final and non-refundable.

For recurring WashDrop plans:
• Cancellation: You can cancel your WashDrop subscription at any time through the app settings. Your benefits will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
• Refund Eligibility: Subscription fees are non-refundable once the billing cycle has started. We do not provide pro-rated refunds for unused washes within a month.
• Failed Payments: If a subscription payment fails, your plan will be suspended until a valid payment method is updated.

To protect the scheduling of our partner workshops:
• 24-Hour Rule: Cancellations made within 24 hours of the appointment time will incur a fee of EGP 70 or 2.5% of the service cost (whichever is lower).
• No-Shows: If you fail to show up for your appointment without prior notice, the workshop deposit or full payment is non-refundable.

• QR Stickers: Refunds are only available for physical stickers if they are returned unopened and unused within 14 days.
• Signals Subscription: Monthly digital subscriptions for receiving messages are non-refundable once billed.

Approved refunds are automatically issued to the original payment method (Credit Card, Digital Wallet, etc.). Please allow 5 to 10 business days for the amount to reflect in your account, depending on your bank's processing time.
