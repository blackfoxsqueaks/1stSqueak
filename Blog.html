You Asked: Is Engine Washing Dangerous?
The engine of your car needs to be cleaned for a number of reasons. It gives an automobile the appearance of being well-kept and cared for, which might increase the resale value. In addition, some experts claim that an engine can run cooler if it is clean and free of grease, oil, or other contaminates. In other words, it may contribute to the reduction of overheating. Today, we’ll investigate if cleaning an engine actually has any mechanical advantages.
